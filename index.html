Vexor Privacy Policy Last Updated: April 24, 2025 Summary: Vexor is built with privacy at its core. We don't collect your data, your wallet private keys remain on your device, and we don't track your browsing or transaction activity. Introduction Vexor ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our Chrome extension. Information Collection We do not collect or transmit any of your personal data to our servers. All user data, including: Wallet private keys Transaction history Portfolio information Security settings and PIN codes User preferences remains stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage mechanisms and is never transmitted to any remote servers controlled by us. External Services Vexor connects to the following external services: Blockchain RPC endpoints (Ethereum, PulseChain, BSC, Base) to execute transactions and verify contract information Price data providers (such as DexScreener) to display token values Blockchain explorers to verify contract details These connections are made solely to retrieve necessary data for the extension's functionality. When connecting to these services, only the minimum information required to process your requests (such as wallet addresses for balance checks or contract addresses for verification) is transmitted. Local Storage Vexor uses your browser's local storage mechanisms to maintain: Your wallet information (including encrypted private keys) Your active wallet selection Security configuration including PIN protection settings Lists of purchased and removed tokens User interface preferences This data remains on your device and persists between browser sessions to provide a seamless experience. Security Measures We take several measures to protect your data: Automatic security locking when tabs become inactive Optional PIN code protection for sensitive areas of the extension Local-only storage of sensitive information Permissions Required Vexor requires several Chrome permissions to function properly: Storage: To save your wallet information and preferences locally ActiveTab: To detect contract addresses on webpages you visit Tabs: For security features and cross-tab communication Scripting: To scan dynamic content and add interactive elements Host Permissions: To connect to blockchain services and price APIs Analytics and Tracking Vexor does not include any analytics or tracking systems. We do not monitor your usage of the extension, the websites you visit, or the transactions you perform. Children's Privacy Vexor is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this privacy policy from time to time.
